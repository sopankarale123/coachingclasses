Regular reports on student progress

We keep regular records of our students’ work, monitoring every lesson with a personal planner. Each student is given a planner along with their designated subject folder. Our teacher also has a planner for each individual student under their guidance. We monitor homework with a cover sheet where a parent’s signature is required after the work has been marked and recorded by the teacher. Our teachers are instructed to take assessment tests every four weeks, outlining all the topics covered in the previous lessons. After such thorough monitoring, every 8 weeks we provide the parents with feedback and a progress report updating them with the child’s progress.
